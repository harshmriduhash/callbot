The launch is not a single day; it's a 3-stage process designed to minimize bugs and maximize social proof.

## Stage 1: The "Invisible" Beta (Days 1-20)
- **Goal**: Find 5-10 "Design Partners."
- **Action**: Manually setup the AI for businesses you know. Do it for free.
- **Outcome**: Fix the "weird" things the AI says and get 3 video testimonials.

## Stage 2: The "Gated" Launch (Days 21-35)
- **Goal**: 20-50 users.
- **Action**: Launch a landing page with a "Join Waitlist" button. Post "Call Demos" on social media daily.
- **Outcome**: Validated demand and a list of eager prospects.

## Stage 3: The Public Explosion (Days 36-45+)
- **Goal**: 100+ users.
- **Action**: Launch on Product Hunt, Hacker News, and Indie Hackers. Open the "Growth" payment tier.
- **Outcome**: Self-serve revenue and scalable growth.
